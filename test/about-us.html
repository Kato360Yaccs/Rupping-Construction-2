About Us

Rupping Construction

Rupping Construction is a Stellenbosch-based building and construction company serving clients across the Western Cape. With over 30 years of experience, the company specialises in reliable residential and commercial construction, including new home builds, renovations, alterations, additions, extensions, granny flats, commercial upgrades, and specialist finishing work. The company focuses on quality workmanship, clear communication, hands-on site management, and a professional building experience from planning through to handover. Rupping Construction also offers added expertise in home automation, lighting design, and audio-visual integration, helping clients plan modern, high-end spaces with clean, practical finishes. Their services include: Residential construction Commercial construction Home renovations and remodels Additions and extensions Outbuilding construction and conversions Granny flats and studios Construction finishes and upgrades Compliance and certification support Home automation and AV integration
